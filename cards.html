Pizza

A delicious and versatile dish made from a round, flat base of dough, typically topped with tangy tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a variety of ingredients such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. Baked to perfection, pizza has a crispy crust and a gooey, flavorful center that makes it a universally loved comfort food.
